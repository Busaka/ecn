Support and Training

These services provide personalised, one on one mentoring sessions, to support your unique role and needs within your service.

Onsite mentoring and workshops are available to you and your team. They can be delivered in the comfort of your own workplace at a time most suited to you.

What are the benefits of in-house training?

The benefits of having an in-house training workshop from Early Childhood News include but not limited to the following;

  • all educators receive the same message and information at the same time
  • the workshop information can be specific to your context and needs
  • it is a comfortable and familiar environment where educators should be comfortable to
  • speak up to clarify their understandings
  • stops misinformation or 'childcare whispers' infecting your service practice and
  • embeds a consistent and informed approach.
